Our Departments

01

CSE

Computer Science & Engineering: CSE is the study of the theoretical foundations of information and computation. It focuses on the software-hardware interface, algorithm design, and software development.Software development, Operating Systems, Database Management (DBMS), Networking, and Theory of Computation

02

EEE

Electrical and Electronics: Pioneering the future of intelligent systems and automated reasoning.EEE is a broad field that covers everything related to electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It ranges from large-scale power generation to tiny electronic components.

03

CSD

CSE (Data Science): Turning vast amounts of data into actionable insights and intelligence.CSD focuses on the intersection of computer science and the creative arts. It integrates standard engineering principles with design thinking to create user-centric digital products.

04

IT

Information Technology: Managing and securing the infrastructure of the modern world.While often confused with CSE, IT focuses more on the application and management of technology within an organization. It’s about using computing tools to solve business problems.

05

ECE

Electronics & Comm: Innovations in telecommunications, robotics, and hardware design.ECE bridges the gap between hardware and communication. It deals with the design and development of electronic devices, circuits, and communication equipment
